# Google Drive Integration

Google Drive integration allows you to store and manage files directly in your Google Drive account from within Frappe.

---

## Features

1. **File Uploads**: Upload files directly to Google Drive.
2. **File Management**: Organize and manage files in your Google Drive account.
3. **Authentication**: Securely connect your Google account using OAuth.

---

## Setting Up Google Drive Integration

1. Go to **Integrations** > **Google Settings**.
2. Enable Google Drive and configure the OAuth credentials.
3. Authenticate your Google account to start using the integration.
